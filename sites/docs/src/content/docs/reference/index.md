Where the [guides](/how-it-works/) explain and the [recipes](/recipes/) and
[examples](/examples/) demonstrate, these pages **specify**: each is the
exhaustive treatment of one surface, accurate to the source it documents.

- **[CLI](/reference/cli/)** — `validate` and `init`, every flag, output
  format, exit code, and the config auto-discovery order.
- **[Declarative YAML](/reference/yaml/)** — the complete `mcVersion: 1`
  authoring vocabulary: frontmatter fields, the body grammar, content leaves,
  text constraints, and corpus routing.
- **[Library API](/reference/api/)** — every runtime export of the package,
  grouped by role, with signatures.
- **[Typed model](/reference/model/)** — the `Doc` that `read()` returns:
  frontmatter, body navigation, tables, and anchors.
- **[Findings & rule IDs](/reference/findings/)** — the finding shape, the
  three severity levels, and the full catalog of engine-emitted rule ids.
- **[Dialect](/reference/dialect/)** — the Obsidian constructs the parser
  understands: `^block-id` anchors, `[[wikilinks]]`, and transclusions.
- **[Glossary](/reference/glossary/)** — the load-bearing terms, defined once
  and cross-linked.
